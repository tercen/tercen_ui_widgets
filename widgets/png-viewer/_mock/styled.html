Drawing Tool Reference

Polygon Click vertices. Click near first vertex (or double-click) to close.
Rectangle Drag to define bounding box. Release to commit.
Circle Drag from centre. Radius = drag distance.
Arrow Drag tail to head. Filled arrowhead rendered at endpoint.
Freehand Drag to trace a free-form path. All sampled points stored.
Text Click to place. Inline input appears. Press Enter or click away to confirm.
